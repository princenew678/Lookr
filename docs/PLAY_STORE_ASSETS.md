Lookr – the AI fashion app that shows you how any outfit looks on YOU before you buy.

Stop guessing. Stop returning. Start Lookr-ing.

Upload your photo, share any product from your favourite shopping app, and Lookr's Nano Banana AI instantly shows you exactly how it looks on your body — in seconds.

🔥 ONE-CLICK VIRTUAL TRY-ON
Share any product from Amazon, Myntra, Ajio, Flipkart, Zara, H&M — see it on you instantly. Every outfit, every store, every time.

📸 TRY REAL-WORLD ITEMS
Snap a photo of clothes in any physical store and try them on virtually. Walk out only with pieces you know you'll love.

🪆 MANNEQUIN TO MODEL (NEW)
Transform boring flat mannequin product photos into stunning realistic shots. See exactly how it drapes on a real body.

🎨 VIRAL AI AESTHETICS (NEW)
Generate viral fashion edits in seconds — Y2K, Dark Academia, Cottagecore, Old Money, Streetwear, Coquette, and more. Go viral effortlessly.

🖼️ AI PHOTO EDIT
Auto-enhance, relight and stylize your fashion photos with one tap. Magazine-quality, instantly.

👗 SMART WARDROBE
Save your favourite items, organise by category, and let AI mix & match outfits for any occasion.

AI analyses your body shape and recommends the perfect size across Zara, H&M, Levi's, Myntra, and more.

Personalised outfit advice tailored to your body type, lifestyle, and style preferences.

Privacy Policy for Lookr — Last updated: March 2026

Lookr ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy.

WHAT WE COLLECT
When you use Lookr, you may upload photos of yourself and clothing items.
These images are sent to Anthropic's Nano Banana AI for real-time processing
only. They are NOT stored on our servers or shared beyond Anthropic.

HOW WE USE IT
To provide virtual try-on, AI fashion analysis, and style recommendations.

DATA STORAGE
Images are processed in real-time and not retained. Your wardrobe and
try-on history are stored locally on your device only. Clear them anytime
from Profile > Settings.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
Anthropic Claude API (Nano Banana): https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

CHILDREN
Lookr is not directed at children under 13.
